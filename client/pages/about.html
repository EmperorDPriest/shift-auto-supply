Our Approach

Why We Do Manual Payment Verification

When we started, we heard too many stories of customers losing money to fraudulent auto parts sellers online — and of legitimate sellers dealing with fraudulent chargebacks from payment processors.

Our manual payment verification model solves both problems. Every transaction is personally reviewed. Every order is confirmed before it ships. This isn't a workaround — it's a deliberate choice to protect everyone involved.

We know it requires a bit more patience than a one-click checkout. We believe that patience is worth it — and hundreds of satisfied customers across 50 countries agree.
